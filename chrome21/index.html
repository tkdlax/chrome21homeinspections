What We Offer

More Than a Standard Inspection

Most inspectors stop at the basics. We don't. Every service below is available as a standalone or add-on — because the more you know before closing, the better your position.

New Home Warranty Inspection

Your builder's warranty typically expires at the one-year mark. Before it does, we walk the entire home to document everything that should be fixed on the builder's dime — not yours. Don't let the clock run out.

Ask about this →

Pre-Drywall Inspection

Once the drywall goes up, what's behind it stays hidden for decades. This is your only window to inspect framing, electrical rough-in, plumbing, and HVAC installation before it's all sealed in. Critical for new construction buyers.

Ask about this →

Radon Testing

Radon is the second leading cause of lung cancer in the US — and Colorado has some of the highest radon levels in the country. It's colorless, odorless, and undetectable without a test. We include certified radon testing so you know what you're breathing before you move in.

Ask about this →

Sewer Scope Camera Inspection

The sewer line running from the house to the street isn't covered in a standard inspection — and a failed line can cost $10,000–$25,000 to repair. We run a camera through the line and show you exactly what's there: roots, cracks, bellies, blockages.

Ask about this →

Thermal Imaging

Infrared cameras see what the naked eye can't — hidden moisture intrusion, missing insulation, electrical hot spots, and energy loss behind walls and ceilings. We use thermal imaging to find the problems other inspectors walk right past.

Ask about this →

New Construction Pre-Closing Inspection

New doesn't mean perfect. Builder inspections pass homes that have real defects — incomplete work, improper installations, code violations. We give you an independent set of eyes before you sign so the builder fixes it, not you.

Ask about this →
